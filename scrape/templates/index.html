What Is CPM ?

High CPM

CPM stands for Captures Per Minutes ,It basically determines the script to track price of the product per second.

Normal CPM

CPM stands for Captures Per Minutes ,It basically determines the script to track price of the product per hour.

Low CPM

CPM stands for Captures Per Minutes ,It basically determines the script to track price of the product per day.
